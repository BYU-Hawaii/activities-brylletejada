The Philippines is an archipelago in Southeast Asia, consisting of over 7,600 islands. It has a population of about 109 million people. The capital city is Manila, and the largest city is Quezon City. The official languages are Filipino and English.

The Philippines has a diverse culture influenced by Spanish, American, and indigenous traditions. Its economy is one of the fastest-growing in Asia, with key industries including agriculture, manufacturing, and services, particularly business process outsourcing. The Philippines is known for its rich biodiversity, beautiful beaches, and vibrant festivals.
